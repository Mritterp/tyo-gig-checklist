Privacy Policy — Gig Checklist

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Gig Checklist ("the app") is operated by Matt Ritter. This policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data storage

All gig data, checklists, and library content you create in the app is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you enable the optional sync feature.

Optional sync

If you choose to subscribe to the sync feature ($1/year), your email address is used to authenticate your account. Your checklist data is stored securely to enable sync across your devices. You may cancel at any time, after which your data remains on your devices but will no longer sync.

Analytics

The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind. No usage data is collected or shared with third parties.

Third-party services

The app is hosted on GitHub Pages. No other third-party services have access to your data.
